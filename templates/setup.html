Setup

Adium

  1. Download and install the Adium client from http://adium.im/

  2. Open Adium and go to File > Preferences. Click the Accounts tab and then click the '+' in the lower left-hand corner of the window to add an account. From the dropdown list, choose XMPP

  3. For 'Jabber ID' enter [your-username]@vine.im and your password in the password field.

  4. Click 'OK'

  5. You should automatically land back at the Accounts page and if everything went well, your newly created account should turn green.

  6. Close the preferences window and start chatting!
