getting started

To use Vine, sign in using one of the mobile/web/desktop apps listed on the right

by adding a new XMPP/Jabber account with {{ user }}@vine.im and your password.

After connecting, you'll see a new group in your buddy list with your Vine contacts. Enjoy!

For an overview of the basic features, watch this screencast (best viewed at 720p in full screen mode).


adium setup

  1. Download and install the Adium client from http://adium.im/

  2. Open Adium and go to File > Preferences. Click the Accounts tab and then click the '+' in the lower left-hand corner of the window to add an account. From the dropdown list, choose XMPP.

  3. Enter {{ user }}@vine.im in the Jabber ID field and your password in the Password field. If you don't remember your password, you can change it here.

  4. Click 'OK'

  5. You should automatically land back at the Accounts window and, if everything went well, your newly created account should come online.

  6. Close the Accounts window, look for the "{{ user }}@vine.im" group in your buddy list, and start chatting!
