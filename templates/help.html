adium setup

  1. Download and install the Adium client from http://adium.im/

  2. Open Adium, go to the Adium menu in the upper left corner of the screen, and select Preferences.

    Click the Accounts tab and then click the '+' in the lower left-hand corner of the window to add an account. From the dropdown list, choose "XMPP".

  3. Enter {{ user }}@dashdash.com in the Jabber ID field and your password in the Password field. If you don't remember your password, you can change it here.

    Click "OK" in the lower right corner of the window, and not "Register New Account".

  4. You should automatically land back at the Accounts window and, if everything went well, your newly created account should come online.

  5. Close the Accounts window, look for the "Dashdash Contacts" and "Dashdash Conversations" groups in your buddy list, and start chatting!

getting started

  1. Download a chat client app

    • Mac: Adium , Messages/iChat
    • Windows: Pidgin
    • iOS: IM+
    • Web: imo.im

  2. Add your Dashdash account

    Chat clients often support many different services, so look for either XMPP or Jabber .

    Use {{ user }}@{{ domain }} as your username, and use the password you chose when you signed up for Dashdash.

  3. Sign in

    Find the Dashdash groups in your contact list (they might be hidden if no one is online), and send someone a message!

    If you're lucky, the first time you sign in a friendly chat bot named @{{ helpbot }} will say hello.

  4. Enjoy!

    Feel free to email us, or ping @lehrblogger if you need help.
